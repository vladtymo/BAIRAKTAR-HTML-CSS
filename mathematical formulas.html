Mathematical Formulas

Linear Equations

A linear equation is any equation that can be written in the form

ax + b = 0

where a and b are real numbers and x is a variable. This form is sometimes called the standard form of a linear equation. Note that most linear equations will not start off in this form. Also, the variable may or may not be an x so donï¿½t get too locked into always seeing an x there.

Quadratic Equation

The standard form of a quadratic equation looks like this:

ax^2 + bx + c = 0
